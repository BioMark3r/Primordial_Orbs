Overview

Welcome to Primordial Orbs, a fast-paced duel where you sculpt a planet, cultivate life, and hurl cosmic impacts. Each turn is a tight puzzle of plays, impacts, and timing. Your goal is clear and bold: be the first to claim 4 colonization types (Plant, Animal, Sentient, High-Tech).

Quick Start

  1. Choose a planet size and core.
  2. Draw 2 cards.
  3. Place Terraform orbs to build slots.
  4. Impacts target your opponent (unless you say otherwise).
  5. End Play, then Advance to resolve impacts.
Tip Terraform quickly, then pivot into Colonize to race for 4 unique types.

Components

  • Terraform Orbs: build the foundation of your planet.
  • Colonize Orbs: Plant, Animal, Sentient, and High-Tech life.
  • Impact Orbs: destabilize planets or protect your own.
  • Core: your planet's passive ability and weakness.
  • Cataclysm Arena: preview where impacts will land.
Example You can only Colonize on an existing Terraform slot. No Terraform, no colony.

Setup

  1. Pick a planet size (small/medium/large).
  2. Each player chooses a core (Land, Water, Ice, Lava, Gas).
  3. Shuffle and draw 2 cards to start.
Warning Terraform minimums matter: you need at least 3 Terraform on your planet before Colonizing.

Turn Flow

Every turn follows this four-step cadence:

  1. Draw
  2. Play
  3. End Play
  4. Advance

The most important decision is timing: do you build safely, or pressure with an impact?

Actions

Draw

Draw 2 cards at the start of your turn. If your hand exceeds 3, discard down to 3.

Play

During Play you may place up to 2 orbs and 1 impact.

  • Terraform: place onto empty slots to build your planet.
  • Colonize: place onto Terraform slots to gain a colonization type.
  • Impact: strike a target planet to destabilize or clear slots.
Tip If you are close to 4 colonization types, prioritize defense and protect key slots.

Impacts

Impacts are your disruption tools. Target your opponent to break Terraform and Colonize chains, or target yourself to clear unstable slots.

Example If an impact would remove a Terraform slot holding a Colonize orb, the colony is lost too.

Cores

Each core grants a passive and a weakness. Build around it.

  • Land: first Terraform each turn is free.
  • Water: swap two Terraform slots once per turn.
  • Ice: first impact against you each turn is reduced by 1 severity.
  • Lava: your impacts gain +1 severity.
  • Gas: shift-click a hand orb to discard + draw once per turn.

Solo / Tutorial

The in-game tutorial overlay walks you through the fundamentals step-by-step. You can launch it anytime from the ? button.

Tip Use Manual tutorial mode when teaching new players at the table.

Glossary

  • Terraform: a foundational slot that can later be colonized.
  • Colonize: a life type placed on Terraform to score toward victory.
  • Impact: a disruptive orb that removes or destabilizes slots.
  • Advance: the step that resolves impacts and checks for a win.

FAQ

How do I win?

First to collect 4 colonization types (Plant, Animal, Sentient, High-Tech) wins the game. If both players hit four types in the same Advance, the active player wins.

Can I target my own planet with an impact?

Yes. Self-targeted impacts can clear unstable slots or remove a threatened colony to reposition your build.

Is there a hand limit?

Yes. You draw 2 each turn and must end Play with at most 3 cards in hand.

Warning Impacts resolve on Advance, not immediately—plan your defenses accordingly.
